\noindent {\large \textbf{Statement on the Use of Generative AI}}

\bigskip

This manuscript was prepared with the assistance of generative \acrshort{ai} tools, used in a limited and controlled way.
In particular, I used the ChatGPT service provided by OpenAI, via the web interface and the Copilot interface integrated into Visual Studio Code, and the Mistral service provided by Mistral AI, via the web interface.
These tools were used mainly for text-related tasks such as reformulation, grammar and spelling checks, and improving the clarity of sentences that I had written.
All scientific content, results, and conclusions were produced and validated by me and my supervisors.
